About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Houston, focused on building frameworks for responsible and ethical AI. My work is centered on the complete evaluation of both datasets and AI model code, making sure they meet high standards for fairness, privacy, quality, and reproducibility. I have a special interest in auditing clinical and medical datasets, where ethical considerations are so important.

My academic journey started with teaching computer science at Dilla and Wollo Universities in Ethiopia. That experience confirmed my passion for the field and pushed me to pursue advanced research in the United States. Now, as a Research Assistant in the Computational Biomedicine Lab, I'm developing new techniques to improve the integrity of AI systems in real-world applications.

Research Interests

  • Responsible and Ethical AI
  • Fairness and Privacy in AI
  • Dataset and AI Model Auditing
  • Compliance in Clinical Data
