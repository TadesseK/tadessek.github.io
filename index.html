About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of Houston, focused on Responsible and Ethical AI. My work involves building and evaluating frameworks that audit datasets and codebases for fairness, privacy, quality, and reproducibility.

I am skilled in Python, Machine Learning, and navigating clinical data compliance. My goal is to contribute to creating more accountable and transparent AI systems.

Research Interests

  • Responsible & Ethical AI
  • AI Auditing Frameworks
  • Fairness, Privacy, and Reproducibility
  • Machine Learning
  • Clinical Data Compliance
